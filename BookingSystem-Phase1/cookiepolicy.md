# Cookie Policy

## Introduction
This booking system uses cookies to improve user experience, provide essential functionality and analyze site usage. By using this system, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

## What Are Cookies
Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser. They help the system recognize your device and remember preferences.

## Types of Cookies We Use
- **Essential Cookies:** Required for the system to function properly (e.g., login sessions).  
- **Functional Cookies:** Remember user preferences and settings.  
- **Analytics Cookies:** Collect anonymous data to improve the system.  

## Purpose of Cookies
Cookies are used to:
- Maintain user sessions
- Remember language or display preferences
- Analyze usage to improve system performance

## Third-Party Cookies
The system may use third-party services (e.g., analytics providers) which place their own cookies. These cookies are governed by the third party’s privacy policies.

## Data Storage and Retention
Cookies are stored only as long as necessary for their purpose and are automatically deleted after a set period.

## Managing Cookies
You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may limit some functionality of the system.

## Consent
By continuing to use the booking system, you consent to the placement of cookies as described in this policy.
